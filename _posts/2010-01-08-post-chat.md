Abbott: Strange as it may seem, they give cybersecurity professionals very peculiar role names nowadays.

Costello: Funny names?

Abbott: Nicknames, nicknames. Now, on our cyber team we have the Penetration Tester, the Bug Bounty Hunter, the AI Trainer, the Forensic Analyst--

Costello: That's what I want to find out. I want you to tell me the names of the fellows on the cyber team.

Abbott: I'm telling you. The Penetration Tester, the Bug Bounty Hunter, the AI Trainer, the Forensic Analyst--

Costello: You know the fellows' specialties?

Abbott: Yes.

Costello: Well, then who's doing the penetration testing?

Abbott: Yes.

Costello: I mean the fellow's role in penetration testing.

Abbott: The Penetration Tester.

Costello: The fellow doing penetration testing.

Abbott: The Penetration Tester.

Costello: The guy in penetration testing.

Abbott: The Penetration Tester is on that.

Costello: Well, what are you askin' me for?

Abbott: I'm not asking you—I'm telling you. The Penetration Tester is on that.

Costello: I'm asking you—who's doing penetration testing?

Abbott: That's the person's role.

Costello: That's whose role?

Abbott: Yes.

Costello: When you pay off the penetration tester every month, who gets the money?

Abbott: Every dollar of it. And why not, the person's entitled to it.

Costello: The Penetration Tester is?

Abbott: Yes.

Costello: So who gets it?

Abbott: Why shouldn't they? Sometimes their partner comes down and collects it.

Costello: The Penetration Tester’s partner?

Abbott: Yes. After all, the person earns it.

Costello: The Penetration Tester does?

Abbott: Absolutely.

Costello: Well, all I'm trying to find out is what's the person's specialty in bug bounty hunting?

Abbott: Oh, no, no. The Bug Bounty Hunter.

Costello: I'm not asking you who's in bug bounty hunting.

Abbott: The Penetration Tester’s on penetration testing!

Costello: The team has a good AI Trainer?

Abbott: Oh, absolutely.

Costello: The AI Trainer's specialization?

Abbott: Why.

Costello: I don't know, I just thought I'd ask.

Abbott: Well, I just thought I'd tell you.

Costello: Then tell me who's the AI Trainer?

Abbott: The Penetration Tester’s doing penetration testing.

Costello: Stay out of penetration testing! The AI Trainer's specialization?

Abbott: Why.

Costello: Because.

Abbott: Oh, that's our Forensic Analyst.

Costello: Wait a minute. You got a Systems Auditor on this team?

Abbott: Wouldn't this be a fine team without a Systems Auditor?

Costello: Tell me the Systems Auditor's name.

Abbott: Tomorrow.

Costello: Now, when the attacker tries a social engineering trick—me being a good incident responder—I want to trace the event, so I hand off the case to who?

Abbott: Now, that's the first thing you've said right.

Costello: I DON'T EVEN KNOW WHAT I'M TALKING ABOUT!

Abbott: Don't get excited. Take it easy.

Costello: I hand the case to the Penetration Tester, whoever it is investigates, so the attacker pivots to another system. The Bug Bounty Hunter picks up the lead and hands it to the Forensic Analyst. The Forensic Analyst hands it to the AI Trainer. The AI Trainer hands it back to Tomorrow—a full investigation.

Abbott: Yeah, it could be.

Costello: Another incident pops up and it’s a long trace to Forensics.

Abbott: Because.

Costello: Why? I don't know. And I don't care.

Abbott: What was that?

Costello: I said, I DON'T CARE!

Abbott: Oh, that's our Compliance Officer!
